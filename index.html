About us

We are a multidisciplinary lab led by Dr. Derek H. Rosenzweig within Department of Experimental Surgery at McGill University. Our research is focused on musculoskeletal tissue modeling and engineering, predominantly using biofabrication approaches including 3D printing and bioprinting. We currently utilize these tools for tissue engineering applications but also cancer and tissue modeling.

Our interests span biology, materials science, engineering and clinical applications. Visit people for more info!
